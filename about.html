Our Story

A rival school released a whiskey under their name. Garrett Steck was intrigued by the idea and thought, why not Wofford College?

A few months later with the help of Master Blender Dixon Dedman, Steck launched 1854 Reserve, a private label bourbon, as a tribute to his alma mater, Wofford College. 1854 Reserve was a resounding success among Wofford alumni – all bottles for sale online completely sold out within a week and a half.

Steck decided to offer the excitement and engagement of private label bourbon to additional passionate community groups through Southern Specialty Spirits.
